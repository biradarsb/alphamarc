Intelligent Video Solutions

  • Home
  • Products
  • Intelligent Video Solutions

Intelligent Video Solutions” are like EYES with a BRAIN. If your business involves people visiting premises or monitoring secure zones for people movement, then deploying these technological solution can bring in great benefits such as efficiency, cost saving, accuracy, trace-ability and above all Analysis & Reports. In certain cases these solutions could be integrated with existing digital Video Camera / CCTV camera and convert them into a SMART & INTELLIGENT systems. Video Cameras have largely been used for surveillance. However with the new age intelligent video solutions, it is possible to not only undertake surveillance & recording, but also more intelligent functions such as people counting, face recognition, heat maps & dwell analysis, video tracking, zone marking etc. Some of these solutions have advanced & value added features such as alarms or alert systems, analysis reports, programming specific functions etc. These intelligent tech edge solutions provide far more value for the investment made by the organizations.

Product Applications

Benefits

  • Real Time People Counting - Get real time figures of people entering , exiting and total people in the premises.
  • Compare high performing stores or zones – With the data available, identify high performing stores or specific zones in large stores to plan promotional activities.
  • Peak hours – Identify peak hours to plan resource and staff deployment.
  • Video analytics – In a group visiting stores / malls, be able to discern groups which have single purchase decision makers. Helps to plan conversion rate.
  • Secure Zones monitoring – It is possible to monitor secure zones by video analytics and be able to trigger alarms when some one or something enters or exits such zones. Monitoring could be done from CCTV control room for multiple zones, without actually deploying human resources at the site.
  • One Way traffic monitoring - It is possible to mark & monitor “One Way” zones in Large apartment complexes, Corporate office campuses , Industrial complexes etc, while Alarms could be triggered automatically in case of violations.
