These are not just Electronic digital display devices. These are digital display devices with "Intelligence" at the back end which can be programmed centrally. These can be one of the differentiators for customer experience on one hand and efficient & effective operations on the other

Product Applications

Benefits

  • Cost Savings With the capability to change prices with the click of button, there is significant reduction in manual efforts & time saving with automatic price updates thereby saving manpower costs. Store staff could be engaged in activities of higher value of customer service and merchandising. Especially, when there is frequent price variation due to discounts offered by competitors. Also, when discounts are offered for specific period of the day or week or month. With electronic labels, it is easy to execute “right price” & “tailored promo offers” to customers at the right time. Minimizes revenue loss due to inadvertent manual errors in display of revised prices. With superb battery life (some having up to 5 years), the cost of ownership (TCO) reduces.
  • Enhanced Customer Experience Leveraging automation technologies to connect physical store to digital world of electronic labels, smart displays, thermal sensors, computer vision and analytics - retailers could improve shopper experience significantly. QR Code / NFC code can be displayed on the digital tag which can help the customers find more details online instantly. Digital Tags can indicate social rating / consumer rating of products almost real time. Such measures help customers take instantaneous sales decisions, thereby helping in sales conversions & also improve shopping experience. Towards efforts to provide updated and latest information of new products to customers, front ending store staff or POS staff could be provided with large digital display tags.
  • Operational Ease & Operational Efficency With physical stores connected to powerful backend software applications it is possible to make the systems interactive, accurate , outcomes measurable, be able to manage remotely and collaborate with partners in minimal time. The solutions have excellent scalability and are easy to deploy, can manage millions of electronic shelf labels, across numerous stores. Improves overall efficiency of retail processes. Being centrally controlled and monitored, prices displayed are accurate & reliable. It is possible to have dynamic pricing i.e. different price being displayed at different time period. Same label could be programmed for multiple images for display at different time. This feature could be used to display the stock level of product, before store operations. With clearly visible data of stock, store staff can replenish products at appropriate locations quickly helping to prevent stock outs. With all these features it is easy to optimize store management processes and reduce the complaints caused by price errors. For omnichannel retail providers, Electronic shelf labels are the best option to ensure consistency between online and offline prices , as it is possible to make price changes visible simultaneously for both online and offline stores. It is possible to display flashing LEDs on specific products so as to attract customers for new products or promotional offers. As all prices for each tag can be saved as records, it is easy to maintain trail for audit compliance.

Retail

It is an easy, effective and efficient means for signage management in retails stores. Making automatic Price changes across large array of products almost on real time basis, across geographically spread out stores can happen with the click of buttons. It is very efficient solution to manage simultaneous price changes. Differential pricing & differential promotional offers can be managed easily. With years of battery life of the electronic labels (up to 5 years) it is great cost saver.

Corporate Offices

Electronic labels / digitals can be used for room signage, door signage, cubicle signage. meeting rooms / conference rooms signage, delegates signage . With centrally controlled operations, calendar based "content display" is possible. Saves time and effort, ensures accuracy and easy to deploy & manage.

Factories/Industries

Electronic signage can be used for critical production processes, logistics processes, signage of parts, stock level, machine tools, shelves, pallets, warning messages, operational performance figures etc. As there is bidirectional communication between label and the back end software, the changes are almost real time. Different displays / instructions can be accurately, effectively and timely managed, for different shift operations.

Hospitality

In hospitality sector quality and attractive designs are very important factors. Electronic / digital labels not only provide excellent aesthetic looks, but also provide means to control multiple signage centrally. This provides flexibility for quick and accurate changes. It is possible to display signage in different languages for different guests / group of guest at specific time periods. Automatic change over of signage can be programmed for different time period with different content .

Hospitals

Hospitals require signage at various locations – patient names, department names , doctor names, direction signage, equipment signage, public messages etc. Electronic labels are very convenient mode of signage in hospital operations. Signage can be changed any time or can be automatically changed as per predefined content and predefined date / time, as the system can be centrally configured or controlled.

Event Management

Event management often requires display of signage of different sizes & different content. These can be used for signposts, direction pointers, caution messages, temporary messages. Sometimes, there may be a requirement to change the content on the same signage board at short notice. Electronic Display labels are very useful and fulfill all these requirements. With central control feature, signage can be changed any time almost on real time basis and can be configured for different content at different time periods on the same signage board. Unlike paper or plastic based signage, which mostly become useless after the event, electronic labels / digital boards can be reused ( in many cases the battery life is up to 5 years … years of reuse and years of cost saving). These are easy to deploy and enhance efficiency in event management.
