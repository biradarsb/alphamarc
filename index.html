Automation in operations benefits Business Enterprises significantly

With automation it is possible to reduce wastage, achieve higher quality, unmatched accuracy and higher reliability. Automation results in higher efficiency, which could translate to reduction in production / operation costs. Enterprises can plan executing repetitive jobs by machines, while staff could take up work of higher value.
With operations data easily available, further analytics can provide deep insights or foresights for planning and decision making. With these numerous benefits, "automation” helps to keep enterprises competitive.
With the operations data in computer records, traceability is easy. Traceability provides record of events, time stamps of events & event logs which can help in identifications of gaps, planning preventive measures effectively.
