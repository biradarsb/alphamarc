If your business involves people visiting premises "Automated People counting systems” are your EYES with a BRAIN. These systems not only provide count of people, entering or exiting but also provide real time & very valuable information for effective planning, ease of management, optimize resource deployment & enhance operational efficiency

Benefits

  • Real Time People Counting - Get real time figures of people entering , exiting and total in premise
  • Compare high performing area or zones – With the data available, identify high performing stores or specific zones in large retail chains, hospitals, gas stations, malls etc to plan promotional activities, plan resource deployment, infrastructure arrangements etc
  • Peak hours – Identify peak hours to plan resource and staff deployment.
  • Real Time People Counting - Discern groups which have a single purchase decision makers among group. Helps to plan conversion rate.
